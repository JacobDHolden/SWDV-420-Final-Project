Glossary and Terms

Bellow will be a list of terms and definitions.

Bankroll
The amount of money you have to bet with.
Closing Line Value (CLV)
Favorable movment of the line from the oping lines to the closing lines.
Favorite
Team expected to win.
Implied Probability
The chance of the outcome in question happening
Kelly Criterion
Formula for sizing a bet.
Odds
Ratio between the amounts staked by the parties to a bet, based on the expected probability either way.
Opening Lines
First lines that bettors get to place a wager on a particular game.
Spread
Allows bettors to have a simple alternative to the moneyline, while trying to equalize the odds between two teams..
Totals
Combined score of a game.
Underdog
Team expected to lose.
Vigorish, Vig
Fee charged by a bookmaker for accepting a gambler's wager.
